Privacy Policy

SunnyBubbleMatchSorts

Overview

SunnyBubbleMatchSorts is an offline single-player puzzle game. The game is designed to work without account registration, online matchmaking, advertising SDKs, or analytics services.

Information We Collect

SunnyBubbleMatchSorts does not collect, transmit, or sell personal information. The game does not request your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, microphone, camera, or payment details.

Local Data Storage

The game stores gameplay progress locally on your device, including unlocked levels, star ratings, best move counts, and settings preferences such as audio choices. This information stays on your device and is not uploaded to external servers by the game.

Children's Privacy

Because SunnyBubbleMatchSorts does not collect personal information, it does not knowingly gather personal data from children or any other users.

Third-Party Services

SunnyBubbleMatchSorts does not rely on third-party analytics, ad networks, social login systems, or external account services for normal gameplay. If this changes in a future release, this policy will be updated before release.

Data Security

Since gameplay data is stored locally and not transmitted to our servers, privacy exposure is limited to the security of your own device and platform environment.

Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy if the game’s data practices change. Any future update will be reflected on this page together with a revised effective date.
