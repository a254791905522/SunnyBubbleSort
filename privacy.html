Privacy Policy

App: Sunny Bubble Match Sort

Developer: Independent Developer

Effective Date: April 22, 2026

Overview

Sunny Bubble Match Sort is an offline single-player puzzle game. We respect user privacy and are committed to keeping the app simple and safe to use.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, payment information, or account credentials.

Local Game Data

To support normal gameplay, the app may store limited game-related information locally on your device, including:

  • Unlocked levels
  • Star ratings and progress status
  • Music or sound preferences
  • Basic in-game settings

This information remains on your device and is used only to preserve your gameplay experience.

Internet Use

The core gameplay of Sunny Bubble Match Sort does not require an internet connection. The app is intended to be playable offline.

Third-Party Services

The app does not include third-party advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, account login systems, or social networking integrations as part of its core experience.

Children's Privacy

Because the app does not collect personal information, no personal data is knowingly collected from children or adults through gameplay.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share personal data with third parties.

Changes to This Policy

If the app's data practices change in the future, this privacy policy will be updated before those changes take effect.

Support

If you need support related to the app or this privacy policy, please use the official support channel associated with the app's distribution information.
