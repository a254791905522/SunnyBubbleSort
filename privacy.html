Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 20, 2026

Sunny Bubble Sort is designed as an offline single-player puzzle game. This app does not require account registration and does not collect personal information from users.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share personal data such as your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, or payment details.

Local Device Data

The game stores limited gameplay data locally on your device, including:

  • Unlocked levels
  • Star ratings
  • Best move counts
  • Music and sound settings

This data is used only to preserve your in-game progress and preferences.

Internet and Third Parties

Sunny Bubble Sort does not require an internet connection to play. The app does not include third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or social login systems.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children or adults. Since no personal data is collected, there is no personal information to share or sell.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share user data with third parties.

Changes to This Policy

If the app's data practices change in the future, this privacy policy will be updated accordingly before those changes take effect.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact the developer through the support information provided on the App Store listing.
