Sunny Bubble Sort

A bright and relaxing color sorting puzzle designed for quick, cheerful play sessions.

Move colorful balls between tubes, match each tube by color, and enjoy a sunny interface, smooth animation, and gentle puzzle progression. The game is fully offline and easy to understand, while later stages provide a satisfying challenge for puzzle fans.

Game Features

10 handcrafted-style levels with auto-generated solvable puzzle layouts and a clear difficulty curve.

Clean one-tap controls with undo, restart, level select, star ratings, and progress saving.

Sunny visual style with warm colors, soft clouds, and a lighthearted casual puzzle atmosphere.

Offline gameplay with no account system, no online dependency, and no personal data collection.

How to Play

  • Tap a tube to select the top ball.
  • Tap another tube to move the ball if the target tube is empty or the top color matches.
  • Sort every non-empty tube into a single color to clear the level.
  • Use Undo or Restart whenever you want to rethink the puzzle.
